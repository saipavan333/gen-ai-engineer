Build it, and watch it

Two kinds of lab, and they do different jobs — VISUAL labs to see a mechanism run, PYTHON labs to build one. Nothing to install, no account, no API key, nothing to pay. Each sits at the end of its track, but you can jump straight in from here.

VISUAL Watch the mechanism
